EIP-7823: Set Upper Bounds for MODEXP

Introduces an upper bound on the inputs of the MODEXP precompile. This reduces the number of potential bugs and makes it easier to replace using EVMMAX.

Constantsยง

INPUT_SIZE_LIMIT
Each of the modexp length inputs (length_of_BASE, length_of_EXPONENT and length_of_MODULUS) MUST be less than or equal to 8192 bits (1024 bytes).
